Privacy Policy

Overview

MyakuariAI ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All consultation history and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

The App requests your permission before tracking your activity across other apps and websites for advertising purposes, in compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

Data Storage

All user data (consultation history, diagnosis results, bookmarks) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. No data is sent to our servers.

The App is not intended for children under 17.
